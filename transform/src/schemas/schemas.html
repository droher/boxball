    ```

Additional information. This is a grab-bag of informational items that might not warrant a field on their own. The field is alpha-numeric. Some items are represented by tokens such as: "HTBF" -- home team batted first. Note: if "HTBF" is specified it would be possible to see something like "01002000x" in the visitor's line score.
